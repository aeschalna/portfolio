About Me

I graduated in 2023 with a Bachelor's of Science from Arizona State University, 👩‍🎓
Where my favorite areas of research were ~ and continue to be ~ human-machine relationships and ethical AI development.
I endorse posthumanist theory, rejecting anthropocentrism in all forms, but especially in the design of service robots.
Since graduation, I have relocated to Denver, CO where I love spending time in the snow & sun with my boyfriend and our puppy.
When I'm not coding or solving puzzles, I enjoy cooking yummy meals 🥟, traveling 🛩️, exploring 🦋, the mountains 🏔️,
gardening 🌸, reading fantasy novels ⚔️, video games 🎮, and studying geology 🗿.
